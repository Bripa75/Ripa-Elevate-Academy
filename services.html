Subjects & Services

Ripa Elevate Academy offers comprehensive tutoring across a wide range of subjects. Each service is tailored to the student’s current level, learning style, and goals.

📚Elementary (1st–5th Grade)

Fundamental math skills, reading comprehension, grammar, vocabulary, and creative writing exercises designed to build strong foundations.

📝Test Preparation

Targeted coaching for SHSAT, SAT, and NY State exams. Students learn test‑taking strategies, timing techniques, and practice with realistic questions.

📐Middle & High School Math

Courses include Pre‑Algebra, Algebra, and Geometry. We clarify concepts, solve problems step by step, and prepare students for higher‑level math.

🔢College‑Level Math

College Algebra, Pre‑Calculus, and Calculus I–III. Sessions focus on understanding underlying principles and applying them to complex problems.

💻Coding & Cybersecurity

Introductory programming, algorithmic thinking, and cybersecurity basics for students interested in computer science and digital safety.
