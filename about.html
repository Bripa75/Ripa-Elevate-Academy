About Brian

Hi! I'm Brian, the founder of Ripa Elevate Academy. I earned my undergraduate degree in Computer Science & Information Security from John Jay College and am currently pursuing my Master's in the same field.

I've tutored students from 1st grade through college, helping them succeed in reading comprehension, elementary math, and advanced college-level courses like Algebra, Pre-Calc, and Calculus I, II, III.

I believe in personalized learning and step-by-step instruction to build lasting confidence. Every student learns differently, and I adapt my teaching style to match each individual's needs and learning pace.

My approach combines academic rigor with patience and encouragement, creating an environment where students feel comfortable asking questions and making mistakes as part of the learning process.
