Subjects Offered

Comprehensive tutoring services tailored to each grade level and individual learning needs. From foundational skills to advanced coursework, we've got you covered.

Elementary (Grades 2–5)

  • Reading comprehension and fluency
  • Basic arithmetic and word problems
  • Multiplication and division
  • Introduction to fractions
  • Writing and grammar fundamentals
  • Study skills and organization

Middle School (Grades 6–8)

  • Pre-Algebra and algebraic thinking
  • Advanced reading and literature analysis
  • Essay writing and research skills
  • Decimals, percentages, and ratios
  • Multi-step problem solving
  • Test-taking strategies

High School

  • Algebra I & II
  • Geometry and Trigonometry
  • Pre-Calculus
  • Advanced writing and composition
  • Regents and State Exam preparation
  • College application essays

Science (Grades 3–12 & Intro College)

  • Elementary & Middle (3–8): General Science, Life Science (cells, ecosystems), Earth Science (weather, climate, geology, space), Physical Science basics (matter, energy, motion), Scientific Method
  • High School (9–12): Biology (genetics, evolution, ecology, human body systems at HS level), Physics (motion, forces, energy, waves, electricity), Environmental Science
  • Intro College: College Biology, Introductory Physics (algebra-based), Environmental Science, general science electives, and study strategies for labs & exams
  • Extras: Science fair guidance and lab report writing; AP Biology & AP Physics support

Taught by our science specialist currently in dental school. Not offered: organic chemistry, immunology, anatomy, or regular chemistry.

College & Test Prep

  • SAT and SHSAT preparation
  • College Algebra
  • Calculus I, II, and III
  • Discrete Mathematics
  • College-level writing and research
  • Study strategies for higher education

AI & Technology Skills

  • ChatGPT and AI tool mastery
  • Midjourney and creative AI
  • AI productivity tools
  • Basic coding and programming
  • Digital literacy skills
  • Technology for business applications
