Mini-FAQ
What grades is this for? Grades 3–8 for the free beta, with more grades coming soon.
How long does it take? About 40 minutes—roughly 18 minutes for Math and 18 for English.
Is my child’s data private? Yes. Your results are shared only with you and your tutor. Delete anytime.
Can I talk to someone about the results? Absolutely—your report includes a quick link to schedule a call.
